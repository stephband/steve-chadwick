Performer, studio musician, composer, arranger, educator.

About me

I grew up in the vicinity of Manchester, UK, and now live on the island of Gotland, Sweden arriving here via Washington DC, London and Berlin. I studied for a degree in music in Salford in the 90's and have worked professionally as a musician (and all that entails) ever since. Folk, jazz, big band, funk, pop, orchestral, improv etc..etc...

Other instruments: alto sax, trombone, accordion, percussion, ukulele (all to different standards but not at the same level as my trumpeting, yet!)
